State Firearm Laws

A project on gun safety regulation in America

Laws By Category

We categorized the 112 provisions identified through our research into 14 categories, based on their intended impact on gun safety regulation. You can read more about each of these categories and the provisions that they contain by reading through our About the Categories page.

State Analysis

Each of the 50 states has unique patterns of law enactment and repeal as related to gun violence prevention. We have compiled this information into our State-By-State page and fact sheets, where you can view laws by year alongside their corresponding firearm-related homicide and suicide rates. You can also access links to current statutes on these pages.

Law Data

Through our research, we have been able to create an interactive database from which researchers can access and download data for their own work. In addition, our data pages use maps and graphs to visualize trends over time and by type of law. Check out our Law Data pages to learn more about national trends in gun safety policy.
