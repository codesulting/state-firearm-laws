A project on gun safety regulation in America

Law Categories

We classified the 133 firearm law provisions in our database into 14 categories, based on their intended impact on gun violence reduction. You can read more about the historical context and legal scope of these categories by reading through our About the Categories pages.

State Analysis

Each of the 50 states has a unique history of firearm policy. We have compiled this information into our State-By-State pages and fact sheets, where you can view laws by year alongside their corresponding firearm-related homicide and suicide rates.
